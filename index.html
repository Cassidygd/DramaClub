Intro:

Welcome to MADrama! We seek to provide an outlet for students of Madison Academic Magnet High School to express themselves through the performing arts. We produce at least two main-stage productions a year and several smaller pieces for classroom production. Our drama club is also an integral part of the production of plays here at Madison Academic.
