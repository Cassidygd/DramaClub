History of Madison Drama

  • 2004 - 2006

    In 2004, our Madison Theatre students participated in a one-act play competition in Clarksville. They performed Baker's Dozen, which is a coming of age story about a family in the 1960's. The eldest son is plagued by the threat of polio. In 2005, students performed their highly successful Little Shop of Horrors. In 2006, students performed The Cover of Life. It takes place in Louisiana where 3 women live with their mother-in-law while their husbands, the three Cliffert brothers, are at war. In the spring, the Theatre Department put on the play, Er, which was about a day in the lives of emergency room doctors.

  • 2007 - 2008

    In 2007, Miss Fly directed the two woman one-act play "Patio" to competition in Cordova, Tenessee. The cast and crew of this production were qualified for the state competition in Johnson City, Tenessee. During the spring, they performed the musical, The Apple Tree. This performance featured a huge cast and told three stories. The first was a twist of Adam and Eve, the second a forbidden love story, and the third a 60s-era Cinderella story, all told through music. In Spring of 2008, the Madison Theatre Department performed Grease which was a highly anticipated musical.

  • 2016 - 2017

    In 2016, students performed Emergency Room which was full on comedy, wit, and talent. In the fall of 2017, they performed Drive-In and that performance was a total hit!

  • 2018 - 2019

    In the spring of 2018, they Madison Drama crew performed their successfulshow, A Blade in the Dark. In that same year during the fall, the Department hosted many 10 minute plays which was very successful. In the spring of 2019, students performed an apocalyptic Romeo and Juliet.In the fall, the drama crew performed Shrek the Musical and sung their hearts out! You could tell from the stage design to the costumes that the students put all of their effort into the performance.
